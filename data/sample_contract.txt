VENDOR SERVICE AGREEMENT (SAMPLE)

This Agreement is entered into between Northwind Logistics Pvt. Ltd.
("Client") and Apex Cloud Solutions Inc. ("Vendor") for the provision of
cloud infrastructure hosting and support services.

1. TERM
This Agreement shall commence on April 1, 2026 and continue for an initial
term of 24 months, automatically renewing for successive 12-month periods
unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least 90 days
before the end of the then-current term.

2. SERVICES
Vendor shall provide cloud infrastructure hosting, including compute,
storage, and network resources, with a guaranteed uptime Service Level
Agreement (SLA) of 99.9% measured monthly. Vendor shall also provide 24/7
technical support with a maximum response time of 4 hours for critical
incidents and 24 hours for non-critical incidents.

3. FEES
Client shall pay Vendor a monthly fee of $48,000, invoiced on the first
business day of each month, payable within 30 days of invoice date. Late
payments beyond 30 days accrue interest at 1.5% per month. Fees are subject
to an annual increase capped at 5%, effective on each anniversary of the
Effective Date.

4. SLA CREDITS
If monthly uptime falls below 99.9%, Client is entitled to a service credit
of 5% of that month's fee for each full percentage point of downtime below
the SLA threshold, up to a maximum credit of 30% of the monthly fee. Service
credits must be claimed in writing within 60 days of the relevant month-end
or are forfeited.

5. DATA SECURITY AND COMPLIANCE
Vendor shall maintain SOC 2 Type II certification throughout the term and
provide Client with the current certification report annually. Vendor shall
encrypt all Client data at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.2
or higher. In the event of a data breach affecting Client data, Vendor must
notify Client within 72 hours of discovery.

6. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement for material breach if the breach
remains uncured 30 days after written notice. Client may terminate for
convenience with 180 days' written notice, subject to a termination fee
equal to 3 months of the then-current monthly fee. Upon termination, Vendor
shall provide a full export of Client data within 15 business days at no
additional cost.

7. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Vendor's total liability under this Agreement, except for breaches of the
data security obligations in Section 5, shall not exceed an amount equal to
12 months of fees paid by Client in the preceding 12-month period. Neither
party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware,
without regard to its conflict of law provisions. Any disputes shall be
resolved through binding arbitration in Wilmington, Delaware.
